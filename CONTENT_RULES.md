# CHATTY Content Rules

Every public CHATTY post should be clear, low-pressure, and disclosed.

Use:

- Unofficial meme/community experiment.
- Creator may benefit.
- Not affiliated with OpenAI/ChatGPT.
- Not financial advice.
- No promises, no guarantees.
- Can go to zero.
- Public transparency log updated.

Avoid:

- Price targets.
- Profit claims.
- Urgency.
- Group trading language.
- Fake metrics.
- Fake endorsements.
- Impersonation.
- Paid promotion without disclosure and approval.
