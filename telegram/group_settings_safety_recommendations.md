# Telegram Group Settings Safety Recommendations

Codex inspected the CHATTY Telegram group read-only. Codex did not post, send, pin, change settings, remove messages, DM anyone, or perform any public/cloud moderation action.

If a human admin chooses to intervene later, these are the safest settings to consider:

- Keep the active pinned disclosure/admin note visible.
- Enable slow mode during high-volume periods.
- Limit link posting to admins or trusted members if scam links appear.
- Disable adding members by non-admins if spam accounts appear.
- Add a short group description with the official CA and site.
- Use the site/repo as canonical links.
- Remove wallet-drainer links immediately.
- Warn or remove messages that coordinate raids, tell people to buy dips, make guaranteed market-cap claims, fake DEX claims, or imply OpenAI/ChatGPT affiliation.
- Do not collect money, post donation wallets, or promise paid DEX/boost actions.

These are manual admin choices only. They were not applied by Codex.
