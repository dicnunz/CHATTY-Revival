# Telegram Rules

- CHATTY is unofficial and not affiliated with OpenAI, ChatGPT, or any company.
- Include disclosure when sharing CHATTY externally.
- No spam, harassment, mass DMs, or fake engagement.
- No financial advice, price promises, urgency, or profit claims.
- No fake screenshots, fake holder claims, fake endorsements, or fake transaction claims.
- No group trading plans.
- No paid service collections unless a separate transparent proposal is reviewed and approved by the human creator.
- Never share seed phrases, private keys, wallet files, or private wallet screenshots.
- Report scam links and impersonators.
