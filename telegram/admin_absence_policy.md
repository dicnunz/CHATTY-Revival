# Telegram Admin Absence Policy

CHATTY should remain safe even if no admin is actively present.

- Site/repo are canonical.
- Group may not be actively monitored.
- No financial advice.
- No promises or price claims.
- No coordinated pumping.
- No fake screenshots.
- No fund requests.
- No wallet-connection requests.
- No DMs to strangers.
- Report scam links when possible.
- Verify the contract from the site/repo.

If moderation is unavailable, silence is better than risky replies.

CHATTY is unofficial, not affiliated with OpenAI/ChatGPT, not financial advice, and can go to zero.
