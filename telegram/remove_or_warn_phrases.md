# Remove Or Warn Phrase List

This file lists examples that should be warned on, removed, or corrected in active-market moderation. These are examples of what not to post as project messaging.

## Coordination Or Spam

- `raid`
- `raids`
- `raid this`
- `coordinated`
- `spam`

## Buy Pressure Or Urgency

- `buy every dip`
- `buy now`
- `ape in`
- `don't fade`
- `don’t fade`
- `send it`
- `sendor`
- `LFG`
- `diamond hand`

## Price Or Market-Cap Hype

- `1M`
- `100k soon`
- `100x`
- `millions`
- `million coded`
- `guaranteed`
- `next stop`
- `moon soon`
- `moon hard`
- `with dex this moon hard`

## False Affiliation Or Risky DEX Claims

- `dev followed by ChatGPT`
- `official launch`
- `endorsed by ChatGPT`
- `official ChatGPT`
- `official OpenAI`
- `ChatGPT mascot`
- `OpenAI mascot`
- `DEX paid officially`
- `listing soon`

## Funds Or Wallet Requests

- `creator rewards`
- `donations`
- `wallet for donations`
- `send funds`

## Safe Correction

Use: CHATTY is unofficial, not affiliated with OpenAI/ChatGPT, not financial advice, no promises, can go to zero. Verify the exact contract and official site.
