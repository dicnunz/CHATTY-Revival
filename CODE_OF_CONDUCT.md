# Code of Conduct

CHATTY community spaces and repo discussions should stay factual, non-harassing, and disclosure-safe.

- No spam, harassment, threats, or impersonation.
- No financial advice, price promises, or coordinated promotion.
- No fake screenshots, fake endorsements, or fake social proof.
- No fund requests or wallet-connection requests.
- No private-key, seed-phrase, password, or payment-data requests.
- Keep CHATTY described as unofficial and not affiliated with OpenAI, ChatGPT, or any other company.

Prefer no response over a risky response.
