# No Promises Policy

CHATTY is an unofficial meme/community experiment. It is not an investment product.

The project rejects:

- Price targets.
- Return claims.
- Buy pressure.
- Coordinated promotion.
- Paid boosts as marketing.
- Fake engagement.
- Fake screenshots.
- Burns, locks, buybacks, listings, or DEX payment promises as marketing.
- Undisclosed promotion.
- OpenAI/ChatGPT affiliation claims.
- Claims that DEX/profile/metadata visibility is an endorsement, promise, safety signal, or price signal.

## Community Conduct During Volatility

Allowed: sharing the official CA, sharing the official site, asking questions, posting memes, reporting scams, correcting fake links, saying do your own research, and reminding people it can go to zero.

Not allowed: raids, coordinated buying, telling people to buy dips, guaranteed price/market-cap claims, `1M guaranteed`, fake screenshots, fake DEX claims, impersonation, wallet-drainer links, donation requests, claiming OpenAI/ChatGPT affiliation, or saying DEX visibility guarantees price movement.

DEX/profile/metadata visibility is not an endorsement, not a promise, and not a safety signal. It does not guarantee liquidity, safety, price movement, or future market cap. No Codex payment was made. No boost was verified. No creator payment is verified from the public/free sources checked. Payment source is unknown unless reliable public evidence says otherwise.
