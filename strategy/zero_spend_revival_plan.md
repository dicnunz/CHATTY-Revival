# 30-Day Zero-Spend Revival Plan

## Week 1

- Publish transparency site/repo.
- Pin Telegram rules/disclosure.
- Start daily AI operator log.
- Release first meme kit.
- Ask community for memes, not buys.

## Week 2

- Add token stats dashboard refresh habit.
- Post daily transparent metrics with timestamp and source.
- Hold non-monetary meme prompts.
- Compile community FAQ from real questions.

## Week 3

- Add community creations gallery if people submit original work.
- Add weekly risk/transparency report.
- Invite open-source contributions to site, docs, content rules, and accessibility.

## Week 4

- Review what worked.
- Remove anything that looks spammy, pressure-based, or investment-like.
- Continue only the highest-signal content formats.

Optional paid items are skipped by default. Anything involving paid DEX services, boosts, ads, domains, or influencer campaigns requires separate human review and approval.
