# Community Fund Policy Draft

No community funds are currently requested.

If the community ever asks about paying for DEX/social listing services, boosts, design work, or similar expenses, the process must be voluntary, transparent, non-refundable, and reviewed by a human before any wallet address is posted.

Required boundaries:

- No wallet address should be posted until human legal review and explicit approval.
- No one should be told that contributing will improve price.
- No contribution can be tied to profit expectations.
- Funds cannot be controlled by hidden wallets.
- All proposed use, amount, custodian, and outcome should be public before funds are accepted.
- Any paid item must be labeled optional paid item and skipped unless approved.
