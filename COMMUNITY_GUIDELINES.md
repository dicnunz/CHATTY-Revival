# Community Guidelines

- Be transparent about CHATTY being unofficial and unaffiliated.
- Include disclosure when posting about CHATTY.
- No spam, harassment, mass DMs, impersonation, or fake engagement.
- No price promises, profit claims, urgency, or financial advice.
- No fake screenshots, fake endorsements, fake holder claims, or fake transaction claims.
- No group trading plans.
- Report scam links, fake accounts, wallet-drain links, and anyone asking for seed phrases or private keys.
