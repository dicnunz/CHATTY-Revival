# Community Guidelines

- Be transparent about CHATTY being unofficial and unaffiliated.
- Include disclosure when posting about CHATTY.
- No spam, harassment, mass DMs, impersonation, or fake engagement.
- No price promises, profit claims, urgency, or financial advice.
- No fake screenshots, fake endorsements, fake holder claims, or fake transaction claims.
- No group trading plans.
- No raids, coordinated buying, buy-dip instructions, guaranteed price/market-cap claims, fake DEX claims, wallet-drainer links, donation requests, or OpenAI/ChatGPT affiliation claims.
- DEX/profile/metadata visibility is not an endorsement, not a promise, and not a safety signal.
- Report scam links, fake accounts, wallet-drain links, and anyone asking for seed phrases or private keys.

Allowed: official CA/site sharing, genuine questions, memes, scam reports, fake-link corrections, do-your-own-research reminders, and can-go-to-zero risk reminders.
