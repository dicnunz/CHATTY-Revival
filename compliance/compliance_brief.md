## Rules

- Disclose creator financial interest clearly.
- Do not promise price movement, returns, safety, listings, burns, buybacks, locks, or future utility.
- Do not ask anyone to buy, hold, coordinate trades, or act urgently.
- Do not use fake engagement, fake screenshots, bot swarms, mass DMs, or spam.
- Do not imply affiliation with OpenAI, ChatGPT, Pump.fun, DEX Screener, X, Telegram, or any public figure/company.
- Every public post/page should include the short disclosure or link to the disclosure page.
- Paid boosts, paid listings, DEX payments, and community fund requests require separate human review before anything public is posted.

## Posting Checklist

- Does the post include or link to disclosure?
- Does it avoid price targets and profit language?
- Does it avoid asking anyone to trade?
- Does it avoid affiliation claims?
- Does it avoid urgency, pressure, or group action?
- Is any metric timestamped and sourced?
- Is unavailable data labeled unavailable?
- Would this still read correctly if screenshotted out of context?
